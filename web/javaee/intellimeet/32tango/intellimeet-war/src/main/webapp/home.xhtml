IntelliMeet Welcomes you ... Intellimeet

A web 2.0 portal designed to recruit college freshers. This is a 3 way meeting place for colleges, training organizations and corporates. The corporates can directly recruit from colleges or from training organizations. The candidates can be posted only through colleges or training organizations. All social networking features like voting, discussion etc., can be enabled as addons. Students can find about Colleges, Colleges can promote their graduates, Consultants can project profiles and Corporate can recruit from Colleges and Consultants.

A web 2.0 portal designed on top of the existing social networks for job management. The user registers and creates friends. The user can post jobs only to his profile, and based on the preferences like privacy setting for that particular job, his friends can act upon his job.
